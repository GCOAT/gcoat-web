Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

GCOAT, LLC ("GCOAT," "we," "us," or "our") is a software development and digital consulting company based in the U.S. Virgin Islands. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our website (gcoat.io) or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact and project intake forms:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Company name (optional)
  • Project details, including project type, budget range, timeline, features, existing website URL, inspiration links, and branding status

2. Analytics & Cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with our website. GA4 uses the following cookies:

  • _ga – Distinguishes unique visitors. Expires after 2 years.
  • _ga_* – Maintains session state. Expires after 2 years.

These cookies collect anonymized usage data such as pages visited, time on site, and referral source. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics cookies.

Analytics cookies are only loaded after you explicitly accept them via the cookie consent banner. If you decline, no analytics cookies will be set and no tracking data will be collected. We also respect the Do Not Track (DNT) browser setting. If enabled, analytics will not load even if you have previously accepted cookies.

You can change your cookie preference at any time by clicking "Manage Cookies" in the footer of any page. You can also opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

We also store your cookie preference (accepted or declined) in your browser's localStorage under the key gcoat-cookie-consent. This is not a cookie. It is local to your browser and is never sent to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and project requests
  • Prepare project proposals and statements of work
  • Send confirmation emails when you submit a form
  • Improve our website and services based on usage patterns

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Storage: Form submissions are stored in AWS DynamoDB in the us-east-1 region with encryption at rest enabled by default.
  • Retention: Lead data is retained for 1 year (enforced via DynamoDB TTL), after which it is automatically deleted.
  • Transport: All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).
  • Access: Only authorized GCOAT team members have access to stored data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Service providers: AWS (hosting and data storage) and Google (analytics) process data on our behalf under their respective privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@gcoat.io or akrigger@gcoat.io. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

GCOAT, LLC
Email: info@gcoat.io
Web: gcoat.io
